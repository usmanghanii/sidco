Our Industry Experience & Capabilities

If you need the latest technology in process control instrumentation, the highest quality control valves, and replacement parts, you've come to the right place. At Sidco Engineered Controls we offer everything from the Rosemount 8732 magnetic flow transmitter with in the pipe calibration verification to ABB flow meters, Foxboro pressure transmitters and Fox thermal mass flow meters also with in the pipe calibration verification. You receive carefully selected products to meet your flow, level, pressure, steam, temperature and combustion control requirements.

We offer specialized products such as the Micro Motion Coriolis meter with in the pipe calibration verification, the Rosemount magnetic flow meter and the Badger water meter to name a few. We also provide customized solutions for a wide range of different applications including batch process control, storage tank overfill protection, wastewater effluent flow and pH measurement, and flare gas stack flow at waste treatment plants.

Application:

Power Plant Steam Flow

A critical measurement for a power plant is the amount of steam being sent and billed to its customers.

One of our systems uses a Rosemount vortex flow meter for volume measurement of the steam flow. The meter’s 4 to 20 mA output is sent to a flow computer. A pressure transmitter output and an RTD output are also sent to the flow computer to provide pressure and temperature compensated mass steam flow in pounds per hour.
