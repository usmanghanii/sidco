Our Industry Experience & Capabilities

If you need dependable process control instrumentation, high quality control valves, and replacement parts, you've come to the right place. You receive carefully selected products to meet your flow, level, pressure, steam, temperature and combustion control requirements.


We can bring various manufacturers, such as Rosemount Measurement, Rosemount Analytical Liquid and Gas divisions, Micro Motion, Foxboro, Eurotherm - Chessell, Honeywell and ABB, together to make sure the equipment supplied is the best suited to meet your requirements.


For your projects we've designed and supplied small sub-systems for local process monitoring and alarms, as well as larger systems that include all components, enclosures, drawings, instructions, and start-up assistance, for boiler or process control applications.


We can provide you with new products, product upgrades and retrofits, as well as replacement parts for Fisher, Bailey and other control valve manufacturers.


Repair services and completely remanufactured products are also available for some instruments and valves. In addition to saving you time and money these products are sometimes the only solution for replacing obsolete products which are no longer available from the manufacturer.
